# Privacy Policy for CineScope

**Last Updated: April 10, 2026**

## Introduction

CineScope ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our CineScope mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you create an account:
- **Name**: Your full name for personalization
- **Email Address**: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Password**: Securely encrypted and stored for account access

### Usage Data
We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:
- **Movie Preferences**: Movies you add to your watchlist and favorites
- **Search History**: Movies and content you search for within the App
- **App Settings**: Your preferences including language, theme (dark/light mode), and notification settings
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Log Data**: App crashes, performance data, and error logs

### AI Chat Data
When you use our AI-powered chat feature:
- **Chat Messages**: Conversations you have with the AI assistant about movies
- **Movie Context**: Information about movies you're discussing

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Account Management**: Create and manage your user account
2. **Personalization**: Provide personalized movie recommendations and content
3. **App Functionality**: Enable features like watchlist, favorites, and AI chat
4. **Communication**: Send you important updates about the App (if you've enabled notifications)
5. **Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
6. **Security**: Protect against unauthorized access and fraudulent activities
7. **Language Translation**: Provide multi-language support using AI translation services

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: User passwords are encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods
- **Secure Storage**: Authentication tokens are stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences
- **HTTPS**: All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses secure HTTPS connections
- **Secure Backend**: Our backend servers implement security best practices including helmet.js protection

### Data Retention
- **Account Data**: Stored as long as your account remains active
- **Watchlist/Favorites**: Retained until you remove them or delete your account
- **Chat History**: Stored temporarily and can be deleted at any time
- **Logs**: Automatically deleted after 90 days

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Google Gemini AI
- **Purpose**: Powers our AI chat feature and language translation
- **Data Shared**: Movie information and chat messages
- **Privacy Policy**: [Google AI Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### TMDb API
- **Purpose**: Provides movie information, posters, and metadata
- **Data Shared**: Movie search queries
- **Privacy Policy**: [TMDb Privacy Policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy)

## Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access
You can access your personal information at any time through your profile settings

### Correction
You can update your name and email in the profile section

### Deletion
You can request account deletion by contacting us at support@cinescope.app (all your data will be permanently deleted)

### Data Export
Contact us to request a copy of your data

### Opt-Out
You can disable notifications and other optional features in settings

## Children's Privacy

CineScope is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes without consent

We **MAY** share your data:
- With service providers who help us operate the App (under strict confidentiality)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights and prevent fraud

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

## Cookies and Tracking

The App uses local storage and preferences to:
- Keep you logged in
- Remember your settings and preferences
- Improve app performance

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:
- Posting the new privacy policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending you a notification (if you've enabled them)

Your continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:
- Notify you within 72 hours
- Inform you of the nature of the breach
- Advise you on protective steps to take
