Account Deletion Request

We're sorry to see you go! If you wish to delete your CineScope account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to our support team at: support@cinescope.app
  2. Include in your email:
    • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
    • The email address associated with your CineScope account
    • Your full name (as registered in the app)
    • Brief reason for deletion (optional but helpful)
  3. Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email within 48 hours acknowledging your request.
  4. Processing: Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days.
  5. Final notification: You will receive a final email confirming that your account has been deleted.

⚠️ Important: This Action is Permanent

Account deletion is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will not be able to recover:

  • Your watchlist and favorites
  • Your profile information
  • Your AI chat history
  • Any preferences or settings

All data will be permanently removed from our servers.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Encrypted password
  • Account creation date

App Data

  • Movies in your watchlist
  • Movies in your favorites
  • AI chat message history
  • Search history (if stored)
  • App preferences and settings

Authentication Data

  • Login tokens
  • Session information
  • Account credentials

Data Retention

Immediate deletion: Most personal data is deleted immediately upon processing your request.

Backup retention: For security and compliance purposes, encrypted backups may be retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion. However, your account will be immediately deactivated and inaccessible.

Legal retention: In rare cases, certain information may be retained if required by law, such as for legal proceedings or regulatory compliance. This does not include your personal watchlist, favorites, or chat history.

Alternative: Data Export

If you would like to download a copy of your data before deleting your account, please include this request in your deletion email. We will provide you with:

  • Your profile information (JSON format)
  • Your watchlist (JSON/CSV format)
  • Your favorites (JSON/CSV format)

Data export will be provided within 48 hours, and your account deletion will proceed after you confirm receipt.

Instead of Deleting

If you're considering deleting your account because of a specific issue, we'd love to help! You can:

  • Contact support: Email us about any problems you're experiencing
  • Temporarily stop using the app: Your data will remain saved if you decide to return
  • Clear specific data: You can manually remove movies from your watchlist or favorites within the app
